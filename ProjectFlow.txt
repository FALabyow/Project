1. Add a category in CategoryModule.cs

2. All Categories will be displayed in Category.cs

3. Add a product in ProductModule.cs

4. When adding a product, behind the scene, Stock is also added but with a default value of 0 for ProductQuantity(see Stock.cs in Project.Domain/Entities).

5. All products will be displayed in Product.cs

6. The search for product in Product.cs are filtered by its price, product name, and category name.(To add more search filter, please see OnProductSearched method in ProductPresenter.cs)
